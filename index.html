About First Landing Beach Resort & Villas

First Landing is a 3-star resort, located directly on a sandy beach and offering beautiful views of the nearby Mamanuca Islands. This resort offers traditional bungalow-style accommodations and friendly service. There are 36 spacious ‘bures’ (bungalows) and villas at the resort, all of which have been decorated in a colorful island style. Choose between the garden and ocean bures or the larger 2-bedroom villa which have their own private swimming pools. Breakfast is included in the room rate and is served in the restaurant overlooking the sea. A wide range of international food, including many local dishes and fresh seafood is also offered here throughout the day. Guests at First Landing Beach Resort & Villas will find plenty of activities on offer, including Fijian cooking classes, garden walks and crab racing. Island cruises, snorkeling and dolphin watching can all be arranged too plus there is a children’s club.
